Meeting with Professor Karl Theisen, Arizona State University, USA
On 10th June, Leaders of VNU-UET met Prof. Karl Theisen – Executive Director, Chief Representative of Arizona State University, USA in Vietnam.
VNU-UET’s representatives are Prof. Dr. Pham Bao Son, Vice-Rector; Prof.Dr. Tran Xuan Tu-Head of Division for Science, Technology and Cooperation Development; Dr. Pham Minh Trien, Deputy Head of Division for Science, Technology and Cooperation Development.
Prof. Karl Theisen introduces the cooperation and support programs as management capacity building, quality control of international standard training… which are very useful for VNU-UET. Both sides also discussed the detail contents of MOU.
In conclusion, Dr. Karl Theisen expressed his high appreciation in cooperating with VNU-UET. Both sides will actively discuss for signing the MOU in near future.
