Msc. DINH Thi Thai-Mai
I graduated from Post and Telecommunication Institute of Technology in 2006 and received the Master degree from Paris Sud 11, France in 2008. Since 2010, I have done my PhD study at UET, VNUH. My interests are Computer Networks, Wireless Communications, Advanced Technologies for Next Generation Mobile Communications Networks, especially, Cognitive Radio.

Currently, I work as a Lecturer of Faculty of Electronics and Telecommunications, University of Engineering and Technology, Vietnam National University Hanoi.
