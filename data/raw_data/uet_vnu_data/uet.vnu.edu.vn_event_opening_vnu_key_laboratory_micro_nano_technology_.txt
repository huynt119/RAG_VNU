On 07 November, 2016, VNU-UET opens the VNU Key Laboratory for Micro-Nano Technology. At the ceremony, VNU’s representatives are Prof. Nguyen Hoang Hai, Vice-President, leaders of Organization-Personnel Department and Science-Technology Department. VNU-UET’s representatives are Prof. Nguyen Viet Ha, Rector, other members of Rectorate Board, leaders of functional Divisions, Faculties and research Centers. Leaders of other VNU Key laboratories, leaders of Institute for Material Science and Institute for Physics (VAST), leaders of ITIMS and AIST (HUST), representatives from Faculty of Physics of VNU-HUS and Faculty of Physics of Hanoi National University of Education.
VNU Key Laboratory for Micro-Nano Technology is established with the premise of VNU-UET’s Laboratory for Micro-Nano Technology that was equipped with advanced technical facilities by VNU to conduct researches on multidisciplinary technologies relating to advanced material and Micro-Nano parts and is to develop applied technological products for commercialization and transfer under the direction of VNU and VNU-UET.
At the ceremony, Prof. Nguyen Huu Duc, Vice President of VNU and Prof. Cheolgi Kim, Daegu Gyeongbuk Institute of Science and Technology, South Korea have been received the Decision, which is conferred by VNU leader, to become Co-Director of VNU Key Laboratory for Micro-Nano Technology.
