From 2019 until now, he has continuously been in the top 10.000 most influential scientists in the world and top 100 scientists in the world in the field of Engineering and Technology (ranked 85th in the world). Professor Nguyen Dinh Duc joined the editorial board of a prestigious international journal
Professor Nguyen Dinh Duc is also the first Vietnamese scientist to become a member of the editorial board of the Journal of Aerospace Science and Technology, Elsevier Publishing (Netherlands). The University of Technology, Hanoi National University, said that Professor Nguyen Dinh Duc – chairman of the University of Technology council, vice president of the Vietnam Mechanics Association, director of the Advanced Materials and Structures Laboratory Tien – has just officially become a member of the editorial board of the international journal of aerospace science and technology – Journal of Aerospace Science and Technology, Elsevier Publishing House (Netherlands).
