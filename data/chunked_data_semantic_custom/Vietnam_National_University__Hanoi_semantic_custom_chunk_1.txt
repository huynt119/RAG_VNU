Location
Vietnam National University, Hanoi (VNU) has 7 campuses across 7 districts ofHanoi, namely:
- Cầu Giấy campus
- Hoàn Kiếm campus
- Thanh Xuân campus
- Hai Bà Trưng campus
- Hòa Lạc campus,Thạch Thất district(current headquarters)
- Nam Từ Liêm campus
- Ba Vì campus
