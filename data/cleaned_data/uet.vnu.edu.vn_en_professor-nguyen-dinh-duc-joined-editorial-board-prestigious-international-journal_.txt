Professor Nguyen Dinh Duc joined the editorial board of a prestigious international journal
Professor Nguyen Dinh Duc is also the first Vietnamese scientist to become a member of the editorial board of the Journal of Aerospace Science and Technology, Elsevier Publishing (Netherlands).
The University of Technology, Hanoi National University, said that Professor Nguyen Dinh Duc – chairman of the University of Technology council, vice president of the Vietnam Mechanics Association, director of the Advanced Materials and Structures Laboratory Tien – has just officially become a member of the editorial board of the international journal of aerospace science and technology – Journal of Aerospace Science and Technology, Elsevier Publishing House (Netherlands).
This is one of the leading international journals in the SCI index, top 5%, very prestigious and high quality in the field of science, engineering and aerospace technology.
The magazine’s editorial board members are outstanding scientists in the field of science, engineering and aerospace technology from many countries around the world such as the US, UK, France, Australia, Italy, New Zealand, China… and Vietnam.
Professor Nguyen Dinh Duc is also the first Vietnamese scientist to have this honor.
Professor Nguyen Dinh Duc presented a report at the international conference
Currently, Professor Nguyen Dinh Duc is a member of the editorial board of 10 international journals in the ISI list of reputable publishers, such as:
Aerospace Science and Technology
(Elsevier Publishing); magazine
Mechanical Engineering Science
(Proc. IMechE Part C, SAGE Publishing); magazine
Mechanics of Composite Materials
and magazines
Mechanical Science and Technology
(Springer Publishing); magazine
Applied Mathematics and Mechanics
(Zeitschrift für Angewandte Mathematik und Mechanik, WILEY Publishing); magazine
Cogent Engineering
(UK, Taylor & Francis Publishing); magazine
Science and Engineering of Composite Materials
(De Gruyter Publishing House)…
At the same time, he has published over 350 articles and scientific works, including more than 200 articles in prestigious ISI international journals.
From 2019 until now, he has continuously been in the top 10.000 most influential scientists in the world and top 100 scientists in the world in the field of Engineering and Technology (ranked 85th in the world). in 2023.
For many years now, Professor Nguyen Dinh Duc is the only Vietnamese working in the country ranked by Research.com as the world’s outstanding scientist in the field of Engineering and Technology.
