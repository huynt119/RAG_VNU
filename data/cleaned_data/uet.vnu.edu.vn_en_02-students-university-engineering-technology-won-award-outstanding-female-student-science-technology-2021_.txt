02 students of the University of Engineering and Technology won the award for Outstanding Female Student in Science and Technology in 2021
Overcoming excellent candidates in the field of science and technology to submit their application, the Central Committee of the Ho Chi Minh Communist Youth Union Minh and the Ministry of Science and Technology have selected 20 talented female students to present the Outstanding Female Student Award in the field of science and technology in 2021. In which, the University of Engineering and Technology (University of Science and Technology). Hanoi) has 02 female students of the Faculty of Information Technology, including Bui Khanh Huyen and Duong Ngan Ha, in the list of 20 individuals receiving this award.
This is also the fourth year in a row that the University of Engineering and Technology has had a typical female student achieve this award in the fields of information technology and electronics and telecommunications.
With excellent achievements in learning and scientific research, two female students, Bui Khanh Huyen and Duong Ngan Ha, were voted as one of 20 outstanding female students receiving awards. Specifically:
Student Duong Ngan Ha – President of K64 CACLC3 with the cumulative average of both years of study, reached the top 1 in the field of study; scholarships to encourage study for the second semester of 2019-2020, the first semester of 2020-2021, the second semester of 2020-2021; excellent students in the academic year 2019-2020 and the school year 2020-2021; First prize in the 7th Student Math Olympiad; graduated from VEF’s Machine Learning course; participate in theoretical research projects and technology application projects (Vaccine Allocation Optimization system is in testing phase) at Operation Room Laboratory – ORLab.
Student Bui Khanh Huyen – Member of the Inspection Committee of the School Union, Collaborator of the Union – Association Office, President of the K64 CACLC1 Branch with an overall average score of 3.81/4.0; Second prize at the conference of students doing scientific research at faculty level in the academic year 2020-2021; participated in the CS50 Puzzle Day contest organized by Harvard University (8 out of 10 problems solved); third prize in Shecodes Virtual Hackathon 2021; Second prize at the National Student Physics Olympiad in the academic year 2020-2021; the academic year 2019-2020 won the title of the excellent student, received encouragement scholarships; Vingroup scholarship for the academic year 2020-2021.
It is expected that the award ceremony for the Outstanding Female Student in Science and Technology in 2021 will be held on December 19
th
, 2021, in Hanoi and broadcast live on the portal page of the Central Committee of the Ho Chi Minh Communist Youth Union and the Central Committee of the Ho Chi Minh Communist Youth Union. page Center for Development of Science, Technology and Young Talents.
The Outstanding Female Student Award in the field of science and technology has been jointly organized by the Central Committee of the Ho Chi Minh Communist Youth Union and the Ministry of Science and Technology since 1999 to honor and motivate female students with academic achievements. . modernization of the country.
